Byrom Street Campus

LJMU

Over-cladding of the Byrom Street Campus began with the seven storey James Parsons Building and the ten storey Tower Building. The project was undertaken in 2 phases with a specialist cladding contractor, with Phase 1 completed in March 2010 and Phase 2 completed in September 2010.
