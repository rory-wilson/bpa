Healthcare & Dementia

BPA have a wealth of experience in the design of both new build and refurbishment healthcare projects, in recent years particularly related to good design for dementia. Our clients include various Housing Associations and Care Home providers.

BPA's specialisation in designing effectlively for people affected by dementia stems from a passion for inclusive design. We put the research into practice in the contexts of both new build and refurbishment projects.
