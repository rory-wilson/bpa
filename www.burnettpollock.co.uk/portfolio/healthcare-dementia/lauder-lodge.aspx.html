Lauder Lodge

Lauder Lodge care home near Portobello, Edinburgh opened its doors in December 2017. The building has accommodation for 60 residents with ancillary facilities including a cafe, a library, activity spaces and externally a new car park and a communal garden.

The building is has been designed with a strong emphasis on dementia friendly and inclusive spaces. All ground floor bedrooms have access to the dementia friendly gardens which were designed by our experienced landscape architect.
