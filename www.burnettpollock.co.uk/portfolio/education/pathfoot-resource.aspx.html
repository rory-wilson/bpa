University of Stirling

Pathfoot Resource

The project for the Pathfoot Resource Centre included the upgrading of the exisiting dining room / study area to give a new look as well as the formation of flexible study spaces. A quiet study area with a double glazed partition to the main area and views across the main campus was introduced. Folding acoustic partitions were used to divide the potential spaces to provide maximum flexibility. A new servery and canteen were also built.
