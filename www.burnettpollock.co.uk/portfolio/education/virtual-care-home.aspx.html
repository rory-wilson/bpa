Virtual Care Home

The Virtual Care Home was a collaboration project between BPA Architecture and the Dementia Services Development Centre at Stirling University. Sponsored by the Nominet Trust the Virtual Care Home is an innovative online tool which visually guides people through best practice dementia design. It was shortlisted in the 2012 HSJ Awards in the category for Improving Care with Technology.
