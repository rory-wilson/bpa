Sarah Mullin BArch(Hons) MArch RIBA RIAS

Sarah qualified as an Architect in 2016 having been with the practice since 2014. Sarah studied at the University of Dundee, for her Masters project she designed a new residential complex constructed in timber within a new master plan for the new waterfront regeneration in Dundee. She has experience working on residential, care homes, offices, over-cladding and interior projects. Her interests include sustainability, masterplanning, inclusive design and interior design.
